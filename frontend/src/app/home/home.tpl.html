Welcome to LivingRoomArt!

Find events, create your artist profile and apply for OpenCalls. LivingRoomArt ist the platform for art, without closed communities or higher societies.

Features

Talk to all of your attendees in just two clicks!

All attendees of your event are listed on the right side of the event description, so you have a great overview of the people who are joining.

To address all of them, a single click on "E-Mail attendees" is enough, and you can type a text which supports styling (bold, underline, lists etc.) right out of the box.

Track and manage OpenCalls within the platform!

After creating an OpenCall, artists can apply with a single click. They got offered a text field to put in links and further information.

After receiving the applications, you can view all applicants within a single window and have even further information via the LivingRoomArt profile of the artist! A single Accept/Reject click and the artist gets notified.

Create your CV with links, images and more!

Having to upload, send, or change a file every time you make a small change to your CV is difficult and time consuming. Now, you can always have an updated CV with just one click!

Do you want to be discoverd?

A simple 'Yes' in your profile settings, and you get listed in the artist overview.

Agents, agencies and friends can easily browse through all the artists on LivingRoomArt, and find you.
